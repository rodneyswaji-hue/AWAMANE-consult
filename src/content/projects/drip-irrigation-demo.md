Drip irrigation dramatically reduces water use while increasing yields — but many smallholder farmers have never seen a working system up close. Our demonstration plots close that gap. Farmers visit working installations, learn directly from our agronomists, and leave with a clear picture of the setup costs, daily routines, and returns they can expect on their own farms.

The plots double as training grounds for integrated pest management, mulching, and climate-smart vegetable production.
