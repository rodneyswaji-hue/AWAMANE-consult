After each rainy season, the gabion structures we help communities build accumulate significant sand deposits. Rather than letting this resource go to waste — or be harvested unsustainably — we work with local youth groups to organize controlled, community-sanctioned sand harvesting operations.

This creates direct income for young people, reduces rural-urban migration pressure, and teaches practical skills in resource management, group enterprise, and business basics.
