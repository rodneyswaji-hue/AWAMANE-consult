Mango is one of the most valuable horticultural crops in Eastern Kenya — but yields and fruit quality vary enormously depending on orchard management practices. Our work with mango farmers has focused on establishing well-planned orchards, training farmers in integrated pest management, and improving post-harvest handling to reduce losses and strengthen access to better-paying markets.

Workshops have brought together farmers from across the region to share experiences, learn from research, and connect with buyers and input suppliers.
