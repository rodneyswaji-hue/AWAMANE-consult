Indigenous chickens dominate smallholder poultry farming in Kenya — they are hardy, well-suited to local conditions, and fetch premium prices in urban markets. But productivity remains low on most farms because of preventable issues: housing design, nutrition gaps, and uncontrolled disease outbreaks.

We work with farmers one-on-one and in groups to improve flock management, introduce practical feed formulations, set up basic vaccination routines, and build the record-keeping habits needed to treat poultry as a serious enterprise rather than a backyard afterthought.
