Livestock keeping is a mainstay of many communities across Kenya and Africa — and a critical source of animal protein as global demand continues to rise. But modern livestock farming comes with real challenges: animal welfare, waste management, environmental impact, and market volatility.

We provide expert advice and technical support to farmers and agribusinesses across the entire animal production chain. Whether you run a backyard poultry unit or a commercial dairy herd, we help you optimize operations, meet industry standards, and stay profitable — while keeping sustainability and animal welfare at the center of every decision.

Enterprises that depend on livestock farming in Kenya — including dairy, beef, leather, wool, and feed industries — all benefit from well-managed herds and flocks. Livestock are also a vital source of farm power and manure for crop enterprises, making them central to the integrated farming systems we champion.
